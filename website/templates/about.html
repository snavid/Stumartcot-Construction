Our Company

Building Tanzania's future since 2008

Established Foundation

Stumarcot Company Limited was established in 2008 in Tanzania under the Companies Ordinance (No. 78368). We began our journey with a vision to provide quality construction materials nationwide.

Nationwide Presence

We are a construction company with nationwide presence, having factories and showrooms strategically located in Dar es Salaam, Dodoma, and Mwanza to serve all regions of Tanzania.

What We Do

Comprehensive construction solutions from manufacturing to installation

Manufacturing

We manufacture a wide range of construction materials including tiles of different styles (Cultural, Arabic, English, and Spanish), PCC and RCC interlocking paver blocks.

Fabrication & Installation

Expert fabrication and installation of rolling and sliding gates (automatic and manual), plus high-class welding works for various construction needs.

Equipment Hiring & Supply

Construction equipment hiring services and supply of quality construction materials including culverts, curbstones, and concrete trench covers.

Our Specializations

Quality construction products that meet international standards

Tiles & Blocks

Paving blocks, floor tiles, wall tiles, exterior and interior wall tiles, and mosaic terrazzo tiles in various cultural styles.

Infrastructure Products

Culverts, caravat, curbstones, and concrete trench covers (drain covers) for urban and rural infrastructure development.

Gates & Doors

Rolling shutter doors, automatic and manual gates with professional installation and maintenance services.

Our Values

Professional, Quality and Creativity in everything we do

Quality Excellence

Our team of experts follow stringent quality parameters as per international standards, ensuring every product meets the highest quality benchmarks.

Professional Expertise

Under astute guidance of our leadership, we offer a wide range of products that consistently live up to our customers' expectations and requirements.

Eco-Friendly Approach

We guarantee reliability through personal commitment, precision, and competence, making our products attractive, cost-effective, durable, and eco-friendly.

Our Markets

Serving diverse sectors across Tanzania

Government & Institutions

We operate on wider markets with our main customers being government and non-government departments, as well as various educational and healthcare institutions.

Industries & Individuals

Our products serve industrial complexes, commercial buildings, and individual homeowners, providing quality solutions for every construction need.
