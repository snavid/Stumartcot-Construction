Installation Guide

1
Surface Preparation

Ensure the wall surface is clean, dry, and level. Remove any old tiles, wallpaper, or loose paint. Fill any holes or cracks with appropriate filler.

2
Planning and Layout

Measure the wall and plan your tile layout. Start from the center and work outward. Use chalk lines to mark your starting point.

3
Apply Adhesive

Apply tile adhesive to the wall using a notched trowel. Work in small sections to prevent the adhesive from drying out.

4
Install Tiles

Press each tile firmly into the adhesive, using spacers to maintain consistent gaps. Check alignment regularly with a spirit level.

5
Grouting

After 24 hours, remove spacers and apply grout diagonally across the tiles. Clean excess grout immediately with a damp sponge.

Professional Tips:
  • Always use appropriate safety equipment
  • Allow proper drying time between steps
  • Keep tiles at room temperature before installation
  • Consider hiring a professional for complex layouts
